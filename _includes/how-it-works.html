How it Works

This 4-Month Program follows the LTM Model. During Months 1-3 we will dive deep into one of the Pillars of Connection. All weekly practices and transmissions will focus on a different aspect of the Pillar. Month 4 focusing on Embodied Space Holding & Authentic Facilitation.

Month 1

Connection to the Physical Body

Creating Safety in the Physical Body

Meeting the Body as if for the first time

Discovering what the body is capable of

Cultivating Self Love and Liberated Expression

Month 2

Connection to the Energy Body

Creating Safety in the Emotional Body

Releasing Stagnation and Energetic Transmutation

Embracing the Ugly and Shadow Sourcing

Using the Body as a vessel for Manifestation

Month 3

Connection to the Transpersonal Body

Creating Healthy boundaries in all relationships

Curating intentionality in your environment

Standing in Your Truth and Speaking with confidence

Embodying Authentic Expression

Becoming the Leader of your Life

Month 4

Authentic Facilitation & Embodied Space Holding

How to create and hold space for others

Finding your voice as a Feminine Leader

Learning to guide an Embodied Dance Journey

Building Business the Feminine Way

LTM as a Somatic Healing Modality

This program is designed to be a Holistic Healing experience where we work with the mind-body-spirit connection, integrating mindfulness meditation, physical activation, and spiritual liberation along with theory-based knowledge of somatic healing and embodiment.

This 4-month journey is a time to turn your attention inwards, a time of introspection and connecting deeply with your inner landscape.

Each month, you will be invited to devote yourself to your inner work, prioritize YOU and the process of awakening self awareness at all levels.

You do this by committing to:

  • Daily pre-recorded embodied movement and somatic dance practices
  • Weekly journaling prompts
  • Weekly live group transmissions with Mana Mei & Guest Facilitators
  • Monthly POD Support Calls
  • 1:1 Mentoring calls

Stepping onto this path is stepping into a new way of being. One that prioritizes creating sacred space every day to dive into deep, uninterrupted, vulnerable presence with yourself.

Months 1-3 consists of 3 weeks of content, and 1 week of integration.

The Integration weeks are intended to be a time when you are free to replenish and nourish your self, catch up on missed lectures and practices, and prepare yourself to step into the next month.

Month 4 is a focused training where you will learn to guide an LTM Embodied Dance Journey, as well as the ins-and-outs of Art of Embodied Space Holding & Authentic Facilitation.
