She is the founder of Liberation Through Movement - a lifestyle and personal transformation business that realigns women with their power to step forward as the ultimate creators of their lives through the modality of embodied dance and intentional movement.

Mana is also the Founder of Mana Movement- an embodied movement meditation modality that has already supported thousands of women around the world in cultivating harmonious mind-body-spirit connection and cultivating unwavering inner safety and freedom.

Through her life-long studies of classical dance techniques and over 10 years of exploring non-traditional movement, Mana has discovered the immense healing power of somatic movement and intentional dance, and is passionate about sharing this wisdom with people all over the world.

Mana believes that our bodies are vessels built to explore all of the complexities of the human experience, and by becoming deeply embodied, we can cultivate unwavering inner safety and freedom, and engage in the world from this place, allows us to live in authentic liberation.

Mana has been mentoring women through pivotal moments of their healing journeys in 1-on-1 and and small group programs, and has supported over 100 women as their personal mentor as they rise into self-empowerment and become the master Creatrix of their lives. This embodied engagement allows us to understand better how these patterns operate within us—and, as a result, learn to navigate life with greater awareness and ease.

Mana Movement works on a pre-verbal level, allowing you to integrate experience without needing to define it. The body already knows what is waiting to be released and reintegrated.
