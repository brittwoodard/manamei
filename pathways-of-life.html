This 4-month journey is a time to turn your attention inwards, a time of introspection and connecting deeply with your inner landscape.

Each month, you will be invited to devote yourself to your inner work, prioritize YOU and the process of awakening self awareness at all levels.

You do this by committing to:

  • Daily pre-recorded embodied movement and somatic dance practices
  • Weekly journaling prompts
  • Weekly live group transmissions with Mana Mei & Guest Facilitators
  • Monthly POD Support Calls
  • 1:1 Mentoring calls

Stepping onto this path is stepping into a new way of being. One that prioritizes creating sacred space every day to dive into deep, uninterrupted, vulnerable presence with yourself.

Months 1-3 consists of 3 weeks of content, and 1 week of integration.

The Integration weeks are intended to be a time when you are free to replenish and nourish your self, catch up on missed lectures and practices, and prepare yourself to step into the next month.

Month 4 is a focused training where you will learn to guide an LTM Embodied Dance Journey, as well as the ins-and-outs of Art of Embodied Space Holding & Authentic Facilitation.
