You can begin incorporating these practices into your daily flow at any point in your pregnancy journey, as well as during your postpartum healing, and beyond! Mana Movement will support you at every stage of your life!

Within this course you will find 3 core Mana Movement Practice, each inviting you to explore non-linear movements in different body positions to support increased fluidity and deepen body awareness. There are also 2 supplementary practices- a stretching practice that is recommended for before your Mana Practice, and a bonus Liberation Through Movement Guided Embodiment Meditation to further activate your creativity and open your body to even more magic!

It is recommended to first complete the program in the order that it is offered, then once you have experienced all practices, give yourself permission to choose which practice will best serve you day-to-day.

Listen to your body, allow her to guide you, and use this time to dive deep into your self-devotion, reconnecting with the power and magic of the Feminine Body.
